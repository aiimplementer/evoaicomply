Help & Guide
Where to start, and the steps to reach CMMC readiness
Quick Path
Discovery → upload evidence → let AI classify & map it → fix flagged gaps → pull the report. No integrations to configure — just export from your existing tools and upload.
Step-by-Step
1. Start at Discovery
Answer the AI chat's questions about your systems, cloud providers, and security tools. It uses your answers to set your CMMC scope (Level 1/2/3) and build your org profile automatically.
2. Upload your Evidence
Export what you already have — policy PDFs, config exports, screenshots, audit logs, training records — and drop them into the upload zone. No logins or integrations needed, just files.
3. Let AI Classify It
Uploaded files are automatically sorted into categories (policy, config, log, screenshot, training, inventory). Anything ambiguous is flagged for your review — you only need to step in there.
4. Review Control Mapping
Each uploaded file is linked to the specific NIST 800-171 / CMMC controls it satisfies. Check the matrix to see what's implemented, partial, or missing at a glance.
5. Close the Gaps
Gap Analysis surfaces what's incomplete based on the mapping. Each gap includes a remediation walkthrough and an effort estimate — ask the AI chat for step-by-step fixes if you get stuck.
6. Pull the Readiness Report
A generated summary of your compliance posture, key findings, and remaining risks — ready to export as a PDF for your assessor or internal record.
Good to Know
• Settings is optional throughout — adjust your target CMMC level, toggle AI auto-classification/mapping, or check supported file types there.
• Remediation Tasks collects every fix flagged during Gap Analysis in one checklist, assignable to your IT lead.
• You can come back and upload more evidence at any time from the Evidence Collection page — there's no fixed cutoff before classification and mapping re-run.
